OUR BEGINING

The trust was particularly founded and brought in action in January 2021 by Mrs. Neeki Singh.

Our happy souls wish to cheer up each and every part of the society by providing all the desired benefits , may it be internships or scholarships or even pension schemes.

The organisation is almost entirely run by youth , the budding future.

MISSION

'Loka samasta sukhina bhavantu.' The great line says 'Let the entire world be happy.'

The principles our trust works upon are simple yet profound. We believe in upliftment of caboodle altogether and making our very society jovial than usual.

The trust wholly excepts member participations and better healthy living of individuals.

Thereupon we welcome you to the happy tribe.

VISION

The likability of healthy lifestyle has gradually increased since the covid rife.

Everyone has been on their toes ever since and trying to get fit and boost metabolism.

Therefore we are focusing on helping each one coho comes forth and becomes a part/member.

FOUNDER

FUTURE PLANS

Why you should join us / Goals of our Program:-

1. To develop important life skills in participants such as Leadership, Self Confidence, Team Work, Survival in Tough Conditions, Effective Communication, Problem Solving and Trust etc.

2. To provide mentors who can guide participants towards a focused approach, ethical belief system and clarity of thinking while dealing with challenges in life.

3. To engage participants in activities which develop competitiveness, social & behavioural skills.

4. To promote healthy lifestyle, sports participation, discipline and sense of purpose among the youth.

5. To engage participants in activities which gives them a sense of responsibility and usefulness.

ANS provides amazing volunteering opportunities with certificates, you can also become a yearly or lifetime member and benefit from our training & leadership programs.
