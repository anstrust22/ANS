ANS: Spread Health & Happiness

ANS Charitable Trust is a nonprofit organisation founded in Jan’2021 by renowned fitness expert Neeki Singh. All our programs are guided by the principle “Loka Samasta Sukhino Bhavantu” means “Let the entire world be happy”. We believe that it can be achieved by connecting haves with have nots because on one hand we have lot of people with lot of money and on other hand we have people who do not have enough money to meet their basic needs (food, cloth, shelter, education).
