Society Welfare

Serve Happiness

Celebrations are much more fun when you've got people to share with. ANS charitable Trust helps you to donate food & exigency to the needy.The trust ensures to provide not only the donation but also the love you gift them..

Happy Home

The basic needs of a common man are rightfully described as 'Roti, Kapda, Makan' Many labours & BPLs fail to build themselves a permanent roof. ANS aims to build as many possible houses for such painstaking individuals..

Student Scholarship

Funding education is also a part of welfare. We appreciate registrations and verify academically excellent students who are genuine and determined towards their future at the same time need financial push..

Widow Pension

We are happy to announce our new pension scheme, specially for widowed women. The trust immensely believes in growth of each & every community & wishes to support women empowerment through this initiative..
